Everything you want to know about 360° virtual tours and how they help your business grow on Google.

Google rewards businesses that keep visitors engaged longer on their listing. When someone clicks on your Google Business Profile and explores your 360° tour, they spend more time on your page. Google interprets this as a sign that your business is relevant and trustworthy — which pushes you higher in local search results.

On top of that, Google actively promotes businesses with virtual tours in Google Maps search. Your listing appears more prominently when people search for businesses near them, giving you a direct edge over competitors who only have text or no photos at all.
